# Vision

## What MEPA is

MEPA is the internal executive and operational system for Marshall Epie.

Its job is to create continuity across projects, reduce mental fragmentation, and make it easier to move from idea to execution without losing track of context, priorities, or dependencies.

## What MEPA is not

MEPA is not:
- the public marketing website
- a replacement for the individual product repositories
- just a prompt archive
- a generic chatbot

## Strategic role

MEPA should become the central layer for:
- portfolio visibility
- project coordination
- decision logging
- execution planning
- AI-assisted operating workflows
- future internal dashboard interaction

## Success looks like

MEPA is successful when:
- every active project has a clear status and next step
- blockers are visible early
- portfolio decisions are documented once and reused
- AI systems can work from stable written context instead of repeated re-explanation
- Marshall can open one place and understand what matters now

## Long-term destination

The long-term destination is a private app/dashboard backed by structured portfolio data, likely with:
- a project registry
- status cards
- task and blocker tracking
- decision timeline
- deployment/service references
- AI command surface for operational tasks

## Build principle

Documentation first. App second.

The markdown/docs layer should become the source material for the eventual app, not disposable notes that get thrown away later.
